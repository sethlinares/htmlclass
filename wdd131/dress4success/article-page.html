Are THESE plants toxic to cats?!

The plant known as, Zamioculcas zamiifolia, or ZZ for short, originated in Africa, where it thrived. A couple decades ago, this plant was brought from South Africa to Dutch nurseries and has since been distributed worldwide. These plants are both easy to take care of and has been proven to remove certain toxins such as xylene, toulene, and benzene from the air. Despite the pros, it turns out that these plants have harmful effects such as nausea and diarrhea when consumed by cats.

Which Cat Breeds Are Orange?

Orange cats are often regarded as sweet and and adorable, some even joke about them sharing only one brain cell, but others wonder which breeds of cat can even be orange. Well it turns out that every orange cat is a tabby cat. Each orange tabby cat has distinct markings on their heads that have been passed down for generations by their ancestors.

My Cat Is Meowing Too Much!

Meowing is the primary way cat's communicate with humans. Aside from purring, it is the most common noise cats make. The odd thing is that cats dont meow at eachother once they become adults. This behavior is only known to be done in kittens at an early age.

Do Cats Get Stressed?

While stress may seem like a very human feeling and state of being, it turns out cats can easily be stressed out from a number of factors including other animals, cats, people and their environment. It's extremely important to make sure your cat isn't being overly stressed when traveling or around new pets or people.

What is the White Spotting Gene in Cats?

White spotting is a common thing in all cats alike and it turns out that it occurs in both purebred and random-bred cats alike!
